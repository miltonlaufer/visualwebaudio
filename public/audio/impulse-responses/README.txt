Impulse Response Audio Files
============================

These impulse response files are used for convolution reverb effects.

Source: https://github.com/notthetup/auralizr
Original recordings from: OpenAir Library (https://www.openairlib.net/)

Files:
- chapel.wav    - Spokane Woman's Club chapel (warm, musical reverb)
- mausoleum.wav - Hamilton Mausoleum (epic ~15s reverb tail)
- stairwell.wav - Concrete stairwell (bright, reflective)
- basement.wav  - Small basement room (tight, intimate)

License: These recordings are from the OpenAir library, an academic
database of acoustic impulse responses. Please refer to OpenAir's
terms of use for licensing information.
